What is entropy?

Password entropy measures unpredictability. It's calculated as log₂(poolSizelength). A pool of 95 printable ASCII characters and a 16-character password yields ~105 bits — extremely strong. Patterns and common words drastically reduce effective entropy even if the password looks complex.
