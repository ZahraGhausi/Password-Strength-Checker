    Tips for improving strength
    • Prefer a long passphrase (16+ chars) over a short complex password.
    • Avoid names, dates, seasons, sports teams, and predictable substitutions (e.g., @ for a).
    • Don’t reuse passwords; use a password manager.
    • Enable MFA where possible.
